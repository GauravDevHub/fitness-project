Weight Training

Weight training is a form of strength training that uses resistance from weights to build muscle strength, endurance, and overall fitness. It involves exercises using free weights like dumbbells and barbells, or machines designed to target specific muscle groups. Regular weight training helps improve body composition, boosts metabolism, enhances bone density, and supports better posture and balance. It's beneficial for people of all fitness levels and can be tailored to meet individual health and performance goals.
